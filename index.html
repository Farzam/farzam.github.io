    About Me

    I'm a Research Scientist at Symbolica AI. Previously, I obtained my PhD in theoretical computer science from the University of Washington, where I was fortunate to be co-advised by Shayan Oveis Gharan and James Lee. Before joining UW, I received my B.Sc. degree in computer engineering from the Sharif University of Technology. You can find my CV here.
